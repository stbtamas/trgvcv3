About TRGVC

TRGVC is a UK-based creative brand focused on Music, Gaming and Community. The project brings songs, gameplay, visuals and content together under one clear identity.

Who is TRGVC?

One name. Three pillars.

TRGVC represents a creative world where music, gaming and community support each other. The music gives the brand sound. The gaming gives the brand movement. The community gives the brand purpose.

The goal is to build a recognisable digital identity that connects listeners, viewers and supporters through releases, videos, visuals and social content.
